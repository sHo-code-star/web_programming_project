Metropolitan University, Sylhet, is home to a distinguished faculty across its seven departments, each dedicated to providing a top-tier education and fostering student success. The faculty members are experts in their respective fields, blending academic knowledge with industry experience to prepare students for future careers or advanced studies.



  1. Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE): The CSE department is staffed by skilled faculty members with expertise in computer systems, programming, software development, and networking. Their focus is on equipping students with the technical skills required to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

  2. Department of Software Engineering: The Software Engineering faculty includes professionals who specialize in software design, development, testing, and management. They aim to provide students with hands-on experience and knowledge to develop innovative software solutions for real-world problems.

  3. Department of Law: The Law department’s faculty members are well-versed in various branches of law, including constitutional, criminal, international, and corporate law. They guide students to develop strong analytical and critical thinking skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for legal practice and academia.

  4. Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE): The EEE department boasts faculty members with deep expertise in electrical circuits, electronics, telecommunications, and renewable energy. Their goal is to nurture students’ problem-solving and technical skills to meet the demands of the modern engineering world.

  5. Department of English: The English department’s faculty is composed of accomplished linguists, literature experts, and communication specialists. They focus on enhancing students' proficiency in the English language and literature, preparing them for careers in teaching, writing, media, and public relations.

  6. Department of Business Administration: The Business Administration department’s faculty consists of seasoned educators and industry professionals with a wealth of knowledge in management, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, and organizational behavior. They aim to equip students with the leadership skills necessary to succeed in the global business environment.

  7. Department of Economics: The Economics faculty is made up of scholars with expertise in microeconomics, macroeconomics, international economics, and development economics. They encourage students to analyze economic issues critically and prepare them for careers in research, government, and the private sector.

Each department at Metropolitan University is committed to providing an education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also nurtures the practical skills necessary for success in the professional world. Faculty members act as mentors, supporting students in their academic journey and guiding them towards achieving their career and research goals.
