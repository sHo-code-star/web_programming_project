NOTICE & NEWS

The final exam will be place on January 26th.

As the principal of Sylhet Polytechnic Institute, I am pleased to announce that the upcoming semester final exams will be held on January 26th, 2023. I understand that this is an important and stressful time for our students, but I have full confidence in their abilities and their preparation for these exams.

I would like to remind all students of the importance of good study habits and time management in the lead up to the exams. These habits will help you to stay organized, focused, and in control of your workload. It is also important to take care of your physical and mental well-being during this time. Eating well, getting enough sleep, and engaging in physical activity are all essential for maintaining a healthy balance.

I would also like to remind all students of the importance of following the college's academic integrity policy. Cheating or plagiarism will not be tolerated and will result in disciplinary action. Let us all work together to ensure that the exams are fair, and that our students are given the opportunity to demonstrate their knowledge and skills.

In addition, we would like to remind the students to follow the covid-19 protocols in the campus and during the exam to ensure the safety of all students and staff.

I would like to wish all students the best of luck for their upcoming exams. Remember, the only way to succeed is to believe in yourself, stay focused, and give it your best effort. I am confident that you will all do well, and I look forward to seeing the results of your hard work.

Principal, Sylhet Polytechnic Institute
